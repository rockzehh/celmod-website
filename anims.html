|CelMod| Animation

:Starting:

- To start animating, look at an object and type !anim (name) where
(name) is the name you want to give it.
- Once you have the desired position, hit ALT to save the frame. And repeat.

:Keys:

- To move on the X axis, press W or S -- / -- Up Arrow or Down Arrow.
- To move on the Y axis, press A or D -- / -- Left Arrow or Right Arrow.
- To move on the Z axis, press CTRL or SPACE.

- To move the object by position/angles, press the SHIFT key.

- To save a frame, press the ALT key.

:Finishing:

- When you're done with your animation, type !anim in chat.

- Once you've finished the animation, you can view it on any object by
typing !view (name) in chat.
- To loop the animation, type !view (name) #loop.
- If you want to stop an animation, type !view (name) #stop.
- To get rid of an animation, type !anim (name) #clear.
