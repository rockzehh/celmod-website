|CelMod| Effects

These are effects that can be used with !effect/v_effect.

Usage: !effect (effect name) Optional: (loop)

sprite
smoke
hurt
blood
ring
explode
splash
light
core
shake
spotlight
push
fire
vort
bomb

Effects are controlled by color and amt.

Note: Blood, vort, antlion, and bomb may not
appear unless looped (!effect bomb 1)
