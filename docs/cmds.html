|CelMod| Commands

These commands go into the chat. To activate chat press Y on your keyboard.

Click on a category below:

Say Commands

!<prop name> Ex: !blastdoor3
--- Props List
!land <clear to remove>
!save <build>
!load <build>
!freeze
!unfreeze
!del
!del all
!undo
!stand
!smove
!color <color> (all to color all objects)
!paint <color> (all to color all objects)
!fadecolor <color1> <color2> (all to color all objects)
--- Colors List
!amt <transparency value>
!door <door skin>

Extra Say Commands

!switch

All console commands are the same as the say commands
except with a "sm_" instead of "!"

Main Exceptions: !del = sm_remove
!flip|!r|!roll = sm_rotate <flip> <r> <roll>
!paint|!color = sm_color <r> <g> <b>
