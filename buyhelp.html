|CelMod| The Bank Update

:Making Money:

The currency of |CelMod| is referred to as cleers, or in short, CL.

To earn cleers, you can:
- Play and build a lot on the server
- Guessing a prop at the Guess Booth (yellow land square with 3 props inside)
- Sell items that others can't normally get
- Report people using the Dispute System (!dispute)

All objects you buy from others/the store are sent to your bank (!bank)

You can spend cleers on many things such as:
- Objects sold by other players
- Unlockable props
- Unlockable commands
- Emblems(future update)

To view the shop type !shop

If you already have objects you didn't buy (ex: turrets, music, etc.)
Then you can get started by selling them to others who don't have them.

:Commands:

The commands are as follows:
!buy
!sell
!bank

There is much more to come!
